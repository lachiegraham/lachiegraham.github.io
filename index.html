GREGS

a short film competition

You are hereby invited to participate in the GREGS film festival, by creating a short film with a 2-5 minute recommended length (and a maximum length of 10 minutes) to be shown at the GREGS award ceremony and movie premiere.

Participants will be provided with a set of guidelines for their films, and a period of two weeks to create their masterpieces.

Filmmakers and actors may produce/appear in multiple entries within the filming period to be viewed at a black tie movie screening event.
