GREGS

a short film competition

You are hereby invited to participate in the GREGS film festival, by creating a short film with a 2-5 minute recommended length (and a maximum length of 10 minutes) to be shown at the GREGS award ceremony and movie premiere.

Participants will be provided with a set of guidelines for their films, and a period of two weeks to create their masterpieces.

Filmmakers and actors may produce/appear in multiple entries within the filming period to be viewed at a black tie movie screening event.

FAQ

Filming may begin when the themes are announced, February 15th. Films are due February 28th at 11:59pm.
This gives you two weeks to do all script writing, filming, and editing required for your film. Use your time wisely :)


  • Teams are decided by you
  • Teams can be of any size
  • You may submit multiple entries
  • Film can be any genre, so long as it is suitably short-film-like and fits the themes provided
  • Films must not exceed 10 mins in length
  • We recommend a length of roughly 3-5 mins

    This whole thing is just for fun, so it's ok if you don't make something worthy of Sundance. So just make something a little bit silly and have fun with it!

    If time is really a concern for you, we suggest you pick a role that won't be too time consuming.
    For example, volunteering to be an actor in films that others have written probably won't take more than a few hours out of your day.
    On the flip side, editing can be a long and tedious task, so only volunteer for this if you're schedule is sufficiently empty :)
